Research

My research revolves around understanding and improving generalization in neural networks, with an emphasis on graphs and large-scale language models.

Graph Distance Metrics for GNN Generalization

Thesis-level project

Studying efficient, theoretically grounded distance metrics on graphs to better understand how GNNs generalize, and to design architectures tailored to these metrics.

  • Connected graph families & spectral properties
  • Links to message passing and expressivity
  • Potential applications in robustness and transfer

Layer Redundancy in Large Language Models

Ongoing

Investigating redundant transformer layers via metrics on hidden representations (cosine similarity, KL divergence, Riemannian metrics like SpecMaps).

  • Layer pruning and lightweight replacement training
  • Experiments on LLaMA, OPT, and BERT models
  • Focus on preserving performance with fewer parameters

Optimization & Monte Carlo Methods

Course & side projects

Work on hyperparameter optimization for neural networks and Monte Carlo simulation of the Black–Scholes equation, connecting stochastic processes to practical modeling.

Selected Projects

Layer-wise Pruning Experiments for LLMs

Code, experiments, and analysis

Systematic removal and replacement of transformer layers in models like OPT and LLaMA, measuring the impact on downstream tasks (e.g., MNLI) and probing representation changes.

View on GitHub (coming soon)

Graph-based Analysis of Hidden State Embeddings

Custom eigenvector pipeline

Building graph structures over token embeddings from various LLMs and studying eigenvalues/eigenvectors to understand geometric and spectral properties of representations.

Hyperparameter Optimization for Neural Networks

Graduate optimization project

Benchmarking optimization strategies for tuning deep models, including methods inspired by vehicle routing and other combinatorial optimization frameworks.

U-Net for Pneumothorax Segmentation

Medical imaging

Applied a U-Net-based model to the SIIM-ACR Pneumothorax Segmentation dataset, focusing on robust training and evaluation in a medical imaging context.
